Welcome to Dr. Dong Xuan′s Webpage!

Dong Xuan is currently serving as a Chair Professor at Shandong University. Prior to this, he was a tenured full professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University in the United States. His research interests encompass Computer Networking, Mobile Systems, and Cyber-Physical Systems. He received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and his Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University. He is a Fellow of the IEEE.

Major Research Projects

       Computer Networking: Xuan′s research in this field is mainly on wireless network connected coverage, real-time communication and overlay networking.

Connected Coverage in Wireless Networks[Slides]

o   Connected 2-dimensional Coverage under Theoretical Settings: 1- and 2-Connectivity [MobiHoc06], 3- and 5-Connectivity [MobiHoc08a], 4-Connectivity [INFOCOM08], and Pattern Mutation [INFOCOM10]

o   Connected 2-dimenstional Coverage under Practical Settings: Sensors with Directional Antennas [INFOCOM11b] and Bounded Deployment Area [INFOCOM13a]

o   Connected 3-Dimensional Coverage: Low Connectivity [MobiHoc09] and High Connectivity [INFOCOM09]

o   Barrier Coverage: Visual Sensors [INFOCOM15a] and Measurement [MobiHoc08b]

o   Mobile Coverage: Mobility-limited Flip Sensors [TMC07, TPDS07]

Besides, Xuan conducted other research projects in computer networking such as real-time communication [ToN04, INFOCOM01, RTSS01]and anycast [TPDS00], and network security and privacy [S&P07, CCS09]. Click here for more about my research publications.

       Mobile Systems [Slides]:Xuan was among the pioneering researchers who viewed the smartphone as a mobile system capable of communication, computing, and sensing for research study.

  • Electronic and Visual Signal Integration: Off-line Object Retrieval[ICDCS17, INFCOM12a] and On-site Object Localization [INFOCOM14, MobiHoc12]
  • Mobile Health: Fall Detection [PerHealth10], and Drunk Driving Detection [Pervasive Health10]
  • Mobile Social Networking and Advertising: TurfCast [INFOCOM12b], P3-Coupon [PERCOM11], E-Shadow [ICDCS11], and E-Smalltalker [ICDCS10]
  • Localization and Tracking: Flash-Loc [INFOCOM16a] and VM-Tracking [INFOCOM15b]
  • Measurement and Security: Human Mobility [INFOCOM17], Privacy-Preserving Crowd-ML [ICDCS15], Information Flow Tracking [INFOCOM13b], HSPA Measurement [MobiHoc10], and Stealthy Video Capturer [WiSec09]

       Cyber-Physical Systems: Xuan has been focusing on Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) in the recent years, specifically on the development of general sports machines and intelligent manufacturing.

o   General Sports Machine: VaRSM [ICCPS22] (Video1, Video2), MV-Sports [INFOCOM18]

  • Intelligent manufacturing: Pulp Molding [ICCPS23] (Video1, Video2)
