Welcome to My Webpage!

I am a full professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. My research interests include Mobile Systems and Computer Networking. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University. I am a Fellow of the IEEE.
